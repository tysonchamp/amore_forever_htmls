Booking Policy

Please review my booking terms to ensure a smooth and transparent process for your special day.

Securing Your Date

A non-refundable booking fee is required to secure your chosen date and time in my calendar. Until this fee is received, the date remains open for other inquiries.

Final Payment

The remaining balance for all services is due no later than 14 days prior to your ceremony date. Invoices will be provided detailing the payment schedule.

Cancellations

In the unfortunate event of a cancellation, the initial booking fee is retained to cover administrative costs and loss of potential bookings. Cancellations within 30 days of the ceremony may require full payment.
