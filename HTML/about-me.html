My Story

A journey of love, words, and a deep belief that every commitment deserves to be honoured.

Hello, I'm Liliana.

From my earliest memories, I have been captivated by the power of words. It was this passion that eventually led me to the most fulfilling role of my life: becoming a marriage celebrant. For me, it's about so much more than just a ceremony. It's about capturing the essence of two people and their unique love story.

I believe that a ceremony should be a true reflection of you—your laughter, your tears, the inside jokes, and the quiet promises. My approach is deeply personal. I take the time to truly know each couple, understanding the little moments that define their bond. From this, I weave a narrative that resonates, creating a ceremony that is as individual as a fingerprint.

When I'm not crafting love stories, you'll find me with a good book, exploring local markets, or enjoying an Aperol Spritz with friends. I bring this same warmth and joy to every ceremony I lead.
